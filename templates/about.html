About WildTrack

WildTrack (wildtrack.org) is a non-profit organization that aims to develop and provide non-invasive and effective monitoring of endangered species in order to reduce biodiversity loss, enable sustainable coexistence of humans and wildlife, and reduce disease transmission from animals to humans.
